About me

I’m a curious, strategic thinker with a deep appreciation for clarity, structure, and meaningful problem-solving. I thrive on asking the right questions—not just to get answers, but to uncover insights that drive smarter decisions and deeper understanding. My journey into the coding world began with a simple desire to build. I wanted to turn ideas into something tangible, something that worked. What started as curiosity quickly evolved into a passion for logic, design, and the elegance of clean code. Along the way, I’ve learned to navigate complexity, debug with patience, and think like both an architect and an artist. Professionally, I’m drawn to roles that sit at the intersection of strategy, technology, and communication. I enjoy breaking down abstract problems, designing intuitive solutions, and collaborating across disciplines. Whether I’m writing code, mapping out a system, or exploring a new concept, I bring a mindset that’s both analytical and creative. I believe that progress begins with better questions—and I’m always asking them.
